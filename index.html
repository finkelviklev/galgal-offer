08 · Index
08

Index

Notes, optional add-ons, and details that didn't fit in the main flow.

A · Optional add-on

Quick branding loop (1–2 weeks)

By default we ship with a clean, modest visual identity that fits the brief. If you want to go deeper on brand — name treatment, logo refinement, full identity system — we can add a focused brand sprint before Phase 1.

B · Content from social platforms

Instagram & TikTok embeds (not scraping)

Scraping third-party social platforms is against their ToS and brittle to maintain. Instead, we use the official oEmbed flow — creators paste a URL, we render an embed. Lightweight, legal, works for any public post.

C · Religious schedule logic

Shabbat & holiday transaction pause

Location-aware pausing of marketplace transactions on Shabbat and Jewish holidays. Complex edge cases (time zones, daylight saving, in-flight transactions) handled in Phase 1. May warrant a rabbinic review before launch.

D · One codebase, three platforms

React Native

iOS, Android, and Web all ship from one React Native codebase. That's why native iOS and Android apps don't appear as a separate phase — they're built from day one alongside web.

E · What's not included

Outside our scope

We provide end-to-end design, brand, and code for product readiness. Not included: go-to-market, marketing, paid ads, influencer outreach, content moderation staffing, legal counsel, accounting, customer support staffing, and ongoing post-launch operations.
